Word Masters

How to Play

Guess the word in 6 tries. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

  • Green means the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  • Yellow means the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Gray means the letter is not in the word in any spot.

A new word will be available every day.
